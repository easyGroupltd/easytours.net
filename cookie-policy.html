User Notice

This site uses cookies to ensure that you get the best experience throughout the site. Please acknowledge that you accept the use of cookies by clicking the "Accept" button located in the cookie bar at the bottom of the page when you first land on this site before proceeding.

 

What are Cookies

A cookie is a file generated when you navigate to a website and is stored on your computer or device to enhance your experience online and provide tailored information.
Cookies are used for remembering something about you so that a number of tasks can be accomplished such as logging into an account or adding items to a shopping cart while you browse.
Some sites use cookies to remember what you do on their site so that relevant advertisements can be displayed. Some of these sites share their cookies so that advertisements on one website can show similar adverts on another.
For more general information on cookies, please visit cookiepedia.co.uk

 

Cookie Usage

This website uses cookies to collect information for a number or reasons to enhance its services. The data gathered is aggregated ensuring that you remain anonymous throughout and does not allow us to identify you personally.

The data collected is accessed using secure online communication technology. Access is limited to relevant individuals of easyGroup, relevant members of staff within the easy family of brands and third-party organisations we collaborate with for general business practices.

The data collected is used for the following reasons:

  • To count, measure and compare the number of users visiting the site
  • Understand variations in user journeys
  • Analyse various sources of traffic
  • Measure and improve the performance of the website
  • Understand which pages and content are most popular to users
  • Store message and form data between pages of the site
